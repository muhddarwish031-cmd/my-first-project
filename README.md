## ðŸ’¡ Design Process

I followed a structured design workflow to ensure the **VORTEX** app is both functional and visually appealing:

- **User Flow & Wireframing:** Mapped out the user journey from the Splash Screen and Login to the main Dashboard and User Profile.
- **Visual Design & Branding:** Applied a modern aesthetic using a purple-to-blue gradient theme to create a premium feel for interior design enthusiasts.
- **UI Components Creation:** Developed reusable components including input fields for authentication, a structured grid system for the Photo Stream, and a comprehensive Profile layout.
- **Interactive Prototyping:** Connected screens using Figma's prototyping tools to simulate real-world interactions, such as clicking a gallery item to view details.
- **UX Optimization:** Integrated an intuitive Bottom Navigation Bar (Home, Post, Profile) to ensure seamless transitions and a user-friendly experience.

## ðŸ“± How to Use the Application

Follow these steps to navigate through the **VORTEX** prototype:

1. **Authentication:**
   - Launch the app to see the **Splash/Login Screen**.
   - Enter your **Username** and **Password** in the provided fields.
   - Tap the **"SIGN IN"** button to enter the dashboard.

2. **Exploring Inspirations (Home):**
   - On the **Home Screen**, browse through the **Photo Stream** gallery.
   - Scroll through the grid to find various interior design categories.

3. **Viewing Design Details:**
   - Tap on any image (e.g., the **"Modern Toilet Decoration"** card).
   - The app will transition to a **Detailed View**, showing a larger image and specific project information.
   - Use the **Back** navigation or bottom bar to return to the main feed.

4. **Capturing or Uploading Content:**
   - Tap the **Center Camera Icon** on the bottom navigation bar to initiate the upload or capture process (simulated).

5. **Managing Your Profile:**
   - Tap the **Profile Icon** (far right) on the bottom navigation bar.
   - View your personal statistics such as **Followers, Following,** and **Likes**.
   - Browse your personal collection under the **"Your Uploads"** section.
   - Use the **"Edit Profile"** or **"Share Profile"** buttons to manage your account.
